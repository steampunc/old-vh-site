Welcome to the Value Hierarchy Quiz

Part 1:

Please drag and drop the following items into the order of your values, from most valued on top to least valued on bottom.

    Please click next when you have ordered them to your satisfaction

    Part 2:

    Beginning here, you will be asked to compare two of the previous values. If you value the one on the left more, press the left arrow key. If you value the one on the right more, press the right arrow key. If you value them equally, press the down arrow key (try to avoid this, though! It makes less good data). There are also vim keybindings, if you want to use those :)

    At the end, it will display your self consistency and a number of other statistics about your values.
